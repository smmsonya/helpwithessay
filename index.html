Ordering flow

Their “How it works” is long; this is the short timeline version.

1

Submit an order

Pick academic level, paper type, deadline, pages, topic, and attach any materials.

2

Automatic writer assignment

System matches an expert based on subject area, qualifications, and prior performance.

3

Secure checkout

Payment goes through the platform; they list cards and PayPal among options.

4

Draft + quality checks

Work is prepared to spec, then reviewed under their internal checks and originality screening.

5

Review, revise, approve

Request revisions via account tools, then approve after verification.

Guarantees & policies

Summaries, not replacements for full policy texts.

  • Up to 3 free revisions, provided the request does not contradict the original instructions.
  • Revision request can be sent before approval; after approval, support contact is required within 7 days.
  • For orders over 20 pages, free revision window extends to 14 days after approval.

Source: official revision policy page.
